## Rating Distribution  
**Foul:**  
0/5 - entirely worthless, offensive and difficult to listen to

**Unenjoyable:**  
10/15 - pointless, bad, but not offensive  
20/25 - leaves a poor impression, very few redeemable moments  
30/35 - does not impact me as a listener, usually a waste of time  

**Passable to Decent:**  
40/45 - passable, does not leave a lasting impact  
50/55 - average overall, or a mixture of great and poor moments  
60/65 - gives me a sense of personal enjoyment  

**Great to Incredible:**  
70/75 - impressive, thoroughly enjoyable  
80/85 - essential listening, worthy of attention  
90/95 - amongst my favourites of all time  

**Pristine:**  
100 - exclusively limited to things which are incredibly personal, changed my outlook on the artform

*To Note:*
- I don't believe in "separating the art from the artist"
- I rate entirely based on personal merit, my attachment and enjoyment
- If an album is marked as listened but not rated, it is likely that I haven't given it my full attention
